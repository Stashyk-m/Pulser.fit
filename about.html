About Pulser

Fitness software is broken. We're rebuilding it.

Every fitness operator — gyms, chains, yoga studios, boxing and martial arts clubs, CrossFit boxes, padel and ping-pong clubs, climbing walls, sports centres — pays for four or five different tools that don't talk to each other, and still can't predict which members are about to quit. Meanwhile, 61% of active people already use AI-driven fitness apps, bringing expectations the software can't meet.

Pulser is the operating system for that problem. One platform for operators. One AI coach for members. A unified data layer that lets the two finally speak.

Principles
