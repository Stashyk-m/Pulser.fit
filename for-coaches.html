What you get

One cockpit for everyone you train.

Client Roster

Every client, every plan, every payment status — one list, one tap. Across every gym they visit.

Programming Studio

Drag-and-drop plan builder with your templates, exercise library and periodisation — delivered straight into their Pulser.One app.

Live Feedback Loop

When a client trains, you see real sets, reps, tempo and form score — powered by the proprietary movement engine inside Pulser.One.

Smart Check-ins

Automated nudges, video reviews and recovery pings, with the AI surfacing who actually needs your attention this week.

Billing & Packages

Online/offline sessions, packs, subscriptions, cancellations — invoices sent automatically. No more chasing.

Gym Marketplace

Connect to gyms on the Pulser network. Your members can train at partner gyms; the gyms can offer you to their members.

How it fits

Built on the same movement engine your clients already trust.

Pulser.Coach plugs directly into Pulser.One (the member app) and Pulser.Gym (the gym OS). Whatever your client does in a session — at home, at the gym, on the road — flows into your cockpit in real time.

No manual logging. No "can you send me a video?" No more flying blind between sessions.
