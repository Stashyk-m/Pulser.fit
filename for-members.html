DeepTech · Proprietary Engine

Pulser sees your movement. Then helps you own it.

The form feedback, risk score and personalised plans in Pulser.One come from our own proprietary movement interpretation layer — IP we built, not a third-party API. It gets better with every session you train.

Cross-Gym Access

One membership. Every partner gym.

Travelling for work? Weekend in a new city? Moved last month? Your Pulser.One membership checks you in at any gym on the network within your tier grade — with your plan, your history and your coach right there with you.

The Experience

The coach you wish you could afford. In your pocket.

360° body scan

One slow rotation with your phone camera. Proportions, posture, composition estimate — without a gym visit.

Real-time form analysis

Point your phone at yourself. Frame-by-frame feedback on squats, deadlifts, presses — powered by our movement engine.

Wearable sync

Heart rate, HRV, sleep, steps from Apple Health, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Android Health Connect — fed into your plan.

Preventive alerts

Overtraining, elevated injury risk, recovery debt — flagged before they become problems.

Cross-gym check-in

Walk into any partner gym in the network within your tier grade — no day pass, no extra app. Your plan follows you.

Your coach, if you want one

Book a human coach from the Pulser.Coach network. They see your form data. Your sessions get sharper.

How It Works

Three steps. Works with or without your gym.

1

Download Pulser.One

Free forever. Works on its own, or as the member app of any gym on the Pulser network.

2

Scan + connect

Body scan, goals, wearable sync, any medical flags. The AI Coach builds your first plan in minutes.

3

Train anywhere

Home, home-gym, travel-gym, coach's gym. Cross-gym access is built in. Your plan adapts to every session.
