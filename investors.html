Moat

Why incumbents can't copy this.

Proprietary DeepTech IP

Pose estimation, biomechanical modelling, injury-risk scoring and periodisation run on our own stack — not a wrapper on a third-party API. The movement interpretation layer is the product; everything else is distribution.

Architecture lock-in

Mindbody and Glofox were built as scheduling tools. Adding DeepTech-grade movement AI requires a unified data layer they don't have. Rebuilding takes years and alienates existing customers.

Multi-source data flywheel

Camera + wearables + user-shared health records feed a richer model with every session across Pulser.Gym, Pulser.One and Pulser.Coach. More data → better predictions → higher retention.

Peer-reviewed science

Validation studies in biomechanics journals unlock Horizon Europe and EHDS grants — non-dilutive funding competitors can't access.

EHDS compliance moat

EU health data regulation (2025/327) goes live from 2026. Retrofitting compliance into legacy systems is a multi-year, multi-million cost. We're built EHDS-ready from day one.

Cross-gym network effect

Every Pulser.One member can train at any partner gym in the same tier. Reciprocal traffic + automated settlement make the network more valuable with every gym that joins.
