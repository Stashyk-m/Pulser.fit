The engine

A proprietary movement interpretation layer.

Pulser is a DeepTech company. Our movement interpretation engine is IP we own — built from peer-reviewed biomechanics research and trained on real session data from the platform. It runs on-device where possible, in the cloud where it needs to scale.

Every tap on the platform feeds back into the engine. The member gets better cues. The coach gets a clearer picture. The gym gets lower churn. The moat compounds.

Vision

Body scan, posture, composition, live form. One phone camera, one proprietary model.

Biomechanics

Rep counting, tempo, ROM, bar path, risk modelling — based on published research.

Wearables

HRV, sleep, activity — Apple Health, Garmin, Whoop, Oura, Android Health Connect.

AI Coach

Built into Pulser.One. Plans, alerts and nudges generated from your own data, not your feed.
