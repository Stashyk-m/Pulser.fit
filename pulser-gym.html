Cross-Gym Network

Members in the Pulser.One app can train at any partner gym of the same grade while travelling — revenue-share and reciprocal access, built in.

Equipment Database

Catalogue every machine, rack and bench with live status (accessible/broken). The AI Coach builds workouts around what's actually available — and visiting members see your floor before they arrive.

Migration Engine

Custom migration scripts for Mindbody, Glofox, Virtuagym and more. Member data, billing, schedules — imported automatically with zero downtime. CSV/Excel fallback for any platform.

DeepTech

A proprietary movement engine, inside your gym OS.

Pulser.Gym ships with the same proprietary movement interpretation layer that powers the Pulser.One member app — real-time body scan, form feedback, injury-risk scoring and personalised programming. Not a wrapper on someone else's model. Our own IP.
