DeepTech · Proprietary

Not a wrapper. A movement engine.

Pulser.One is built on our own proprietary movement interpretation layer — body scan, form scoring, injury-risk modelling, fatigue detection — all running locally on your phone. We own the IP. It is the moat of the entire platform.

Cross-Gym Access

One membership. Any partner gym.

Travelling? Moving? Just curious? Every Pulser.One member can check in at any gym on the Pulser network within their tier grade — no extra apps, no surprise day-passes. Your plan, your coach and your history come with you.

Core Features

Everything your coach would charge for — built in.

Built from scratch with AI in the core, not bolted on as a marketing tagline.

Camera · On-device

Live Movement AI

MediaPipe tracks 33 body keypoints at 30fps — no cloud, no lag. Real joint angles, bilateral symmetry, form score per rep.

Front · Side · Back · Left

360° Body Scan

Rotate once. Get body shape, frame size, composition estimate, posture flags, and a training plan built for your actual proportions.

AI · Predictive · Health Connect

Predictive Injury Detection

AI analyses joint biomechanics, movement asymmetry and Health Connect data (HRV, sleep, resting HR) to predict injury risk before it happens — a 0–100 score with zone breakdown and personalised prevention cues.

500 devices · Android 14+

Health Connect Deep Integration

Reads Fitbit, Samsung Health, Garmin, Pixel Watch — all through one API. 500+ connected devices feed the injury-risk model automatically.

Adaptive · Personalised

Smart Programming

Your workout plan updates based on your body scan, movement history and Health Connect data. No questionnaire guessing.

Charts · History · PRs

Progress & Analytics

Per-exercise form trend, joint accuracy history, personal bests. See clearly that you're actually getting better.

Equipment-Aware

Smart Equipment Matching

The AI Coach checks the gym's live equipment database before building your session — your plan only includes machines and stations that are actually available right now.

Nutrition · AI-Generated

AI Nutrition Plans

Meal plans built from your body scan, goals, and training load. Macros and calorie targets auto-adjust on rest days vs. heavy sessions — no manual calorie counting required.

HRV · Sleep · Load

Recovery Intelligence

Sleep quality, HRV trends, and cumulative training load combined into a single recovery score. The AI auto-adjusts session intensity or prescribes a deload before burnout hits.

Targets · Milestones

Goal Engine

Set a target — lose 5 kg, squat 100 kg, run a sub-25 min 5K. The AI builds backwards from your deadline and adjusts training + nutrition weekly based on real progress.

Community · Compete

Challenges & Social

Weekly challenges, gym leaderboards, and shared milestones. Train solo, compete with friends, or join community events at your gym — all inside the app.

120+ Exercises · Video

Exercise Library

Every exercise comes with video demos, coaching cues, and muscle maps. Tap any movement in your plan to see exactly how it's done and what the AI scores you on.
